INSTANCES OF JOURNALIST THREAT

Data collected from the Global Voices article archive. This visualization is a sample from a larger project with Visualizing Impact, as part of their fellowship with Global Voices, sponsored by the Ford Foundation and the Mozilla Foundation. Each rectangle represents an instance of journalist threat. Hover over a rectangle for more information and filter by country. Click on a rectangle to read the article from which the data was extracted.
