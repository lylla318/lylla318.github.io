Mississippi River Flow Levels

Data specificities: discharge in cubic feet per second; gage height in feet; turbidity, water, unfiltered, monochrome near infra-red LED light, 780-900 nm, detection angle 90 +-2.5 degrees, formazin nephelometric units (FNU). Data collected from USGS Location 07374525 Mississippi River at Belle Chasse, LA. More information available at the usgs.gov site. Visualization inspiration and support from the lab of Dr. Alex Kolker at the Louisiana Universities Marine Consortium [LUMCON].
